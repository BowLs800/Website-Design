Full-Color Dynamic Message Signs

High-visibility, full-matrix LED display system designed for highways, bridges, and managed lanes. Supports NTCIP 1203, full-color graphics, and long-distance legibility in all weather conditions.

Product Overview

Our Full-Color DMS line provides agencies with advanced messaging capability, enabling both text and graphical content at high brightness and resolution.

Designed for harsh environments, these signs feature redundant power supplies, conformal-coated PCBs, intelligent temperature management, and full NTCIP 1203 compatibility.

Ideal for express lanes, toll facilities, reversible lanes, and major freeway corridors.
