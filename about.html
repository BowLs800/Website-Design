About Ledstar

Ledstar Inc. is a North American manufacturer and integrator of LED dynamic message signage, lane control systems, and traffic management display technologies. With decades of experience serving municipalities, highways, and large-scale infrastructure, we bring together engineering expertise, durable manufacturing, and full NTCIP-compliant systems.

Our products are deployed across highway corridors, tunnels, bridges, airports, and industrial installations. We provide end-to-end solutions — including manufacturing, software integration, testing, installation support, and long-term maintenance services.

Our Core Capabilities

Engineering & Design

Custom LED display development, optical design, NTCIP 1203 compliance, environmental testing, and structural integration.

Manufacturing

Precision LED module fabrication, enclosure assembly, QC testing, and ISO-aligned production workflows.

Software & Integration

System configuration, communications integration, embedded controls, and support for LEDMaster Client/Server.
